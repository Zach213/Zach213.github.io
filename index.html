My Story

I began my journey interning for a Congressman, where I loved the broad scope of impact but craved something more real-time and direct. This led me to shadow a surgeon and explore medicine. However, I realized it offered less room for experimentation than I wanted. Seeking a blend of impact, immediacy, and creative exploration, I took on a consulting internship with Deloitte Digital. During this internship, I discovered Google Venture's Sprint book, detailing their one-week process for helping startups validate ideas. I fell in love with the process of falsifying hypotheses to build meaningful products. Inspired, I presented a workshop on the Design Sprint to Deloitte's partners. Returning to campus, I changed my major to Computer Science, built projects, and wrote a thesis on applying Machine Learning to help students choose majors for greater satisfaction. This journey ultimately led me to internships and a full-time role at Microsoft AI, where I continue to combine experimentation, innovation, and impact.
