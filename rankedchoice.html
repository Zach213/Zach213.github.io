About Ranked Choice Bot

The Ranked Choice Discord Bot facilitates ranked choice voting within Discord servers, making it easy for communities to reach fair and democratic decisions.

Key Features

  • Automates ranked choice voting for Discord servers
  • Real-time results and feedback for users
  • Easy setup and management via Discord commands
