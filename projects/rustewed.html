RU Stewed

Recipe sharing for Rutgers students using dining hall ingredients

About

RU Stewed is a play on "RU Screwed" — an inside joke for Rutgers students for when something about being at Rutgers makes your life harder.

I was eating healthier but felt like I didn't have many options in the dining hall. Knew other folks were being creative, so I built a place to share recipes.

Details

Problem

Students want to eat healthier but don't know what they can make with dining hall ingredients.

Impact

Reached a few hundred students. Learned React and building for content curation.
