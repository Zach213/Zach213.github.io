TierListGPT

A new way to communicate preferences to AI through tier lists

About TierListGPT

TierListGPT is a website that lets you create tier lists to communicate your preferences to AI. By ranking items in tiers from S to F, you can help the AI understand your tastes and preferences, enabling more personalized recommendations and interactions across any website. I created it in 48 hours. The next weekend over 48 hours I filmed 5 demos
