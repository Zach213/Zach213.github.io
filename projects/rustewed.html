RU Stewed

A website for Rutgers students to share recipes using dining hall ingredients

About Ru Stewed

Ru Stewed is play on RU Screwed, an inside joke for Rutgers students for when something about being at Rutgers makes your life harder. I was eating healthier but felt like I didn't have many options in the dining hall but knew that folks were being creative.

Demo Photos

Problem

Students are trying to eat healthier or delicious foods but don't know what they can make with dining hall ingredients.

Solution

A website for Rutgers students to share recipes using dining hall ingredients.

Impact

Reached a few hundred students.

Learning

Learned React and building a website for content curation.
