The LX

Forum for upperclassmen to give underclassmen advice at Rutgers

About

Named after the LX bus on Rutgers campus. I had ~5 upperclassmen write posts that I shared with underclassmen friends who found them helpful — and that I selfishly wanted as someone choosing my major.

Built the entire thing using Bubble with a backend database.

My first shipped product! Learned how to build websites, databases, and use no-code tools.
