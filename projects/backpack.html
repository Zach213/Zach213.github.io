Backpack Optimizer

A Chrome extension to optimize your schedule based on your priorities using the knapsack algorithm.

About Backpack Optimizer

The Backpack Optimizer Chrome Extension simplifies the process of selecting and organizing items for travel or daily needs. It's designed for efficiency and customization.

Demo Photos

Problem

Many competing priorities in a day and unclear how to balance them.

Solution

Chrome extension that uses an optimization algorithm to help you balance your priorities to maximize your productivity.

Impact

Helped me balance my college courses with other priorities.
