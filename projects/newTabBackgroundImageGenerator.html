AI Image New Tab Generator

Chrome extension that generates a new background image every day for your new tab

About

I wanted a new background image every day on my new tab page using the same prompt. Found a few models that could generate images from prompts and built this extension.

My Favorite Prompt

"A serene yet powerful scene of a lone mountain climber standing on a peak at sunrise, overlooking an endless horizon of valleys and rivers, symbolizing resilience, growth, and untapped potential. The climber's gear is minimal, highlighting simplicity and purpose. The sky is vibrant with gradients of orange, blue, and purple, with scattered constellations faintly visible, hinting at the limitless possibilities of the universe."
