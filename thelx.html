About The LX

The LX (named after the LX bus on Rutgers campus) is a forum I built for upperclassment to give underclassmen advice. I had ~5 upperclassmen write posts that I posted and shared with underclassmen friends of mine who found them helpful, and that I selfishly wanted as someone choosing my major. I learned a lot about building a website and set up the entire thing using Bubble with a backend database.

Demo Photos

Problem

Underclassmen are unsure what major to choose or lack advice about navigating college at Rutgers, especially when they don't have access to mentors.

Solution

Forum built for upperclassmen to give underclassmen advice.

Impact

Helped ~ 5 students get advice from upperclassmen and explore majors.

Learning

How to build websites, databases, and using no-code tools. My first shipped product!
