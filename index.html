  • Getting things done: Along the way I've developed a knack for getting things done- even when the initial doors might be closed. Whether it was finnessing my way into the Library of Congress when it was shut, convincing marketing to market a feature before it was shipped, convincing user research to let me interview users directly even when that wasn't the normal process, or going skydiving despite a fear of heights, I've learned to embrace challenges and push boundaries.
