TierListGPT

Communicate preferences to AI through tier lists

About

TierListGPT lets people rank items on a tier list to express their preferences to AI. I created it in 48 hours as a way for folks to build their own "algorithms" that developers could use as a universal preference API.

The next weekend I filmed 5 demos, then rebuilt the site a few weeks later into a preference alignment experiment with A/B testing in 4 days.
