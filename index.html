My Story

I started with an interest in politics for its scope of impact but found I wanted direct rather than delayed impact. I tried medicine but found less room for experimentation since stakes were high in a clinical setting. I explored consulting and user research but wanted to be the one iterating. After reading "The Design Sprint" by Google Ventures, I loved the idea of the sprint. I interned at Microsoft, built projects, changed my major to computer science, wrote a thesis on applying ML models to choosing a major, and came back full-time to apply AI to consumer applications for good.
